Meet Your President-Elect

Dept: Computer Science

The Students’ Union Government (SUG) is the heartbeat of the student community. As a student of Umaru Musa Yar’adua University, I have witnessed the passion, resilience, and brilliance that define our campus.

My election to the office of the SUG President is a responsibility I accept with deep humility. I believe the time has come for a leadership that is not only committed to representing students but is now empowered to transform challenges into systems.

A Mandate for Service

I ran for this office because I believe leadership is not a title—it is work, sacrifice, and service to humanity. Now that the mandate has been given, my focus shifts entirely to leaving a legacy of progress, unity, and impactful transformation that future generations of UMYU students will benefit from.

My Core Values

  • Transparency: Open governance and financial accountability.
  • Inclusion: A union that works for every student, irrespective of faculty.
  • Service: Prioritizing student welfare over politics.
  • Innovation: Using modern ideas to solve campus problems.
"The campaign is over. The work begins. Together, we rise."
