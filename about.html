Meet Abubakar Ahmed Karfi

Dept: Computer Science

The Students’ Union Government (SUG) is more than an institution—it is the heartbeat of the student community. As a student of Umaru Musa Yar’adua University, I have witnessed the passion, resilience, and brilliance that define our campus.

My decision to run for the office of the SUG President is rooted in a deep sense of responsibility, service, and a passion for student development. I believe the time has come for a leadership that is not only committed to representing students but also committed to transforming challenges into systems.

Why I Am Contesting

I am contesting because I believe leadership is not a title—it is work, sacrifice, and service to humanity. I want to leave a legacy of progress, unity, and impactful transformation that future generations of UMYU students will benefit from.

My Core Values

  • Transparency: Open governance and financial accountability.
  • Inclusion: A union that works for every student, irrespective of faculty.
  • Service: Prioritizing student welfare over politics.
  • Innovation: Using modern ideas to solve campus problems.
"Together, we can create a legacy that will stand the test of time. Together, we rise."
